## V(으)려면 멀었다 #card
?begin
### Usage
"There is a long way to go to ~"
### Form
Used with verbs
### Restrictions
### Examples
* 다이어트에 성공하려면 멀었어요
	* There is a long way to go to succeed at dieting
* 그래도 한국어를 유창하게 하려면 멀었어요
	* Though, there is a long way to go if you want to speak korean fluently
* 다음 주에 있을 TOPIK 싷
