## 판 #card
?begin
### Usage
판에 ... or 판이다 it means a game/situation/match. setting where something happens... something else.

1. In the situation where... where...
2. you're ... while this is happening?
3. how could you ... when ...
4. we're doing ... and you're ...
### Form
| 과거   | 현재    | 미래   |
| ---- | ----- | ---- |
| 한 판에 | 하는 판에 | 할 판에 |
