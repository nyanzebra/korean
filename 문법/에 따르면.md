## N 에 따르면 #card
?begin
### Usage
Used strictly for **citing a source** of information. Always followed by reported information. See [[에 따라서]] for more as they share much the same grammar points.
### Form
### Restrictions
Only use for citing information, like: news, research, books, etc..
### Examples
* 뉴스**에 따르면** 내일 비가 온대. 
	* According to the news, it will rain tomorrow.

* 연구**에 따르면** 수면이 매우 중요하다. 
	* According to the research, sleep is very important.
### Notes
**ㅁ에 따르면** is rare and stiff — **ㅁ에 따라서** is far more common in formal writing.

**따르면** doesn't naturally combine with **는지에, 느냐에, 는 것에** because it cites a source — you cite a noun or a reported fact, not an open question.

?end
