### Usage
Used when saying X is the deciding factor for Y. Oftentimes emotional or heavy.
### Form
#### 는것
Nominalizes an action with **것**. Most neutral and versatile.

> 네가 어떻게 행동하**는 것에 따라서** 결과가 달라져. _Depending on how you act, the result changes._

> 네가 노력하**는 것에 달려 있어**. _It hinges on you putting in effort._
