## 에 달려 있다 #card
?begin
### Usage
Used when saying X is the deciding factor for Y. Oftentimes emotional or heavy.
### Form
#### 는것
Nominalizes an action with **것**. Most neutral and versatile.

* 네가 어떻게 행동하**는 것에 따라서** 결과가 달라져
  * Depending on how you act, the result changes.

* 네가 노력하**는 것에 달려 있어**
  * It hinges on you putting in effort.
#### 는지
Expresses **whether or not / how** something is the case. Focuses on uncertainty or an open question.

* 네가 오**는지에 따라서** 계획이 바뀌어
  * The plan changes depending on whether you come.

* 얼마나 준비했**는지에 달려 있어**
  * It depends on how much you prepared.

* 어떻게 하**는지에 따라서** 결과가 달라
  * The result differs depending on how you do it.
#### 느냐
Similar to **는지에** but more **rhetorical and emphatic**. Often implies a more serious or weighty choice.

* 어떻게 생각하**느냐에 따라서** 완전히 달라져
  * It changes completely depending on how you think about it.

* 선택을 어떻게 하**느냐에 달려 있어**
  * It hinges on what choice you make.

* 누가 하**느냐에 따라서** 결과가 다르지
  * The result differs depending on who does it.
#### ㅁ/음
Nominalizes with **ㅁ/음**. More **formal and written**. Common in news, official documents, announcements.

* 법이 바뀜**에 따라서** 절차도 달라졌다. 
	* As the law changed, the procedures also changed.

* 수요가 증가함**에 따라서** 가격이 올랐다
  * As demand increased, prices rose.

* 조사 결과가 발표됨**에 따르면** 문제가 있었다
  * According to the announced investigation results, there wer problems._
