## 단/이란 말이에요 #card
?
### Usage
Has two meanings based on intonation, if rising it is roughly 'do you mean that..?' and said because something is hard to believe. The second is more like 'I mean that/I am saying...'
### Form
| - | 동사 | 형용사 |
| 과거 | 갔단 말이에요 | 추우었단 말이에요 |
