## 에 따라 있다 #card
?begin
### Usage
Used to indicated that things are lined up or positioned along X. Not used for decisions or outcomes

Is about the feeling of things being physically or spatially aligned.
### Form
Only with nouns
### Restrictions
The things being spoken about must be static. So running alongside something doesn't work it has to be things that are situationally permanent like mountains or rivers or stuff.

### Examples
* 강**에 따라** 마을들이 있다. 
	* Villages are situated along the river.

* 길**에 따라** 가로수가 있다. 
	* Trees are lined up along the road.
