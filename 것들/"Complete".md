#### 완료 (完了) — Completion of a process/task

- Focuses on a **task or process being finished**
- Often used in formal, administrative, or technical contexts
- _"Done, processed, concluded"_
- 예: 결제 완료 (payment complete), 다운로드 완료, 신청 완료
----
#### 완성 (完成) — Completion of a creation

- Focuses on something being **fully made or built**
- Used when a work, product, or creation reaches its final form
- _"Finished, perfected, fully realized"_
- 예: 소설 완성, 작품 완성, 드디어 완성됐다 (it's finally finished/complete)
- ----

#### 완벽 (完璧) — Perfection

- **Flawless, perfect** — no room for improvement
- 예: 완벽한 사람은 없다 (no one is perfect), 완벽주의자 (perfectionist)

---

#### 완전 (完全) — Complete, total, perfect

- Means **fully, completely, totally** — often used as an adverb in casual speech
- 예: 완전 맛있어! (so delicious!), 완전한 자유 (complete freedom)

---

#### 완치 (完治) — Full recovery

- Used specifically for **recovering completely from illness**
- 예: 완치됐어요 (I've fully recovered)
----
#### Quick comparison:

|Word|Core idea|Typical context|
|---|---|---|
|완료|task is done|admin, tech, processes|
|완성|creation is done|art, writing, building|
|완벽|no flaws|quality, personality|
|완전|totally, fully|adverb, emphasis|
|완치|fully healed|medical|

---

The biggest one to keep straight: **완료** is for _doing_ something, **완성** is for _making_ something.
