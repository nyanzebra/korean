# 요리 동사 (Cooking Verbs)













## 삶다 #card #verb #boil #cook #simmer
?begin
### 뜻
- to boil
- to cook (in water)
### 예
- 계란을 삶았어요.
	- I boiled eggs.
- 감자를 삶고 있어요.
	- I'm boiling potatoes.
?end





## 식다 #card #verb #cool-down #get-cold #chill
?begin
### 뜻
- to cool down
- to get cold
### 예
- 국이 식었어요.
	- The soup got cold.
- 식기 전에 드세요.
	- Eat before it cools down.
?end
