# 의성어 (Onomatopoeia & Sound Words)

## 웅성거리다 #card
?begin
### 뜻
- noisy
- hum
- excited
### 예
- 군중들의 웅성거림이 단속적으로 들려 왔다
	- The racket made by the mob could be heard intermittently
- 웅성거리는 대화 소리.
	- a buzz of conversation
### Tags
#sound #onomatopoeia #crowd-noise #murmuring #buzzing #humming
?end
