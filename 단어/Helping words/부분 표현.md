## 유난 떨다 #card
?begin
### 뜻
- to overreact
- to make a fuss
### 예
- 조금 다쳤다고 유난 떨지 마.
	- Don't make such a fuss just because you got a little hurt
### Notes
- **유난 (有難)** — comes from Sino-Korean, literally meaning "having difficulty/unusualness." It carries the sense of being out of the ordinary or exceptional — but used negatively, as in _unnecessarily_ standing out or making a scene
- **떨다** — to shake, to tremble, but also used idiomatically to mean "to put on" or "to act out." You'll see it in other expressions too, like 멋 떨다 (to show off) or 재롱 떨다 (to act cute)
?end

## 티끌 모아 태산 #card
?begin
### 뜻
- specks of dust gathered become a great mountain
### 예
- 매일 천 원씩 저축하는 게 별거 아닌 것 같아도, 티끌 모아 태산이야
	- Saving 1,000 won every day might not seem like much, but little by little it all adds up
- 매일 조금씩 거짓말했는데, 티끌 모아 태산이라고 결국 큰 문제가 됐어.
	- I lied a little every day, and just like dust gathering into a mountain, it eventually became a huge problem
### Notes
- **티끌** — a speck of dust or tiny particle
- **모아** — gathered/collected (from 모으다)
- **태산** — Mount Tai (a great mountain); used to mean an enormous amount
?end
